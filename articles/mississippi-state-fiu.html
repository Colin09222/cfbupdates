January 29, 2026 • Week 0 Recap

Mississippi State Falls to FIU 27-21 Despite Jacob Clark's Record 435 Passing Yards

In one of the most stunning upsets of Week 0, Mississippi State fell to FIU 27-21 despite a record-setting performance from quarterback Jacob Clark. Clark threw for an absurd 435 yards, but it wasn't enough to overcome the Bulldogs' defensive struggles.

Jeff Lebby's squad put up gaudy offensive numbers but couldn't get stops when they needed them most. Mike MacIntyre's FIU team executed a near-perfect game plan, controlling the clock and capitalizing on Mississippi State's mistakes.

Final Score

FIU: 27

Mississippi State: 21

Jacob Clark: 435 passing yards
