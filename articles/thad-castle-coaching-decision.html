Where Will He Land? Coach Thad Castle's Big Decision Looms

CU
By CFB Updates Staff July 1, 2026 · 9:00 AM ET
4 min read

The coaching carousel has a new headliner, and everybody in the league is watching. Coach Thad Castle is on the market, and four programs have come calling: USF, Tulsa, Coastal Carolina, and Colorado State. Each one offers a very different path to glory — and Castle isn't the type to make a quiet decision.

He already showed up to the "Head Coach Introduction" podium with a commemorative beer bottle from each school lined up in front of him. When a reporter asked which one he'd pick, he reportedly winked and said, "Whichever one's coldest, baby." One of these four is about to get a coach who wins press conferences before he wins games. Let's break down the board.

The Finalists

USF — Sun Belt (Tampa, FL)

Tulsa — American Athletic (Tulsa, OK)

Coastal Carolina — Sun Belt (Conway, SC)

Colorado State — Mountain West (Fort Collins, CO)

USF — The Sleeping Giant in Tampa

The pitch: Location, location, location. Sitting in the heart of Florida's recruiting goldmine, USF gives Castle instant access to talent most Group of Five schools can only dream about. Tampa sells itself to 17-year-olds.

The reality: With that talent access comes pressure. USF fans have been waiting for someone to turn potential into a winner. If Castle wants the highest ceiling — and the brightest spotlight — this is it.

Castle's take: "Beaches, boats, and blitzes. If I take this job I'm doin' film review poolside, no notes."

Verdict: Highest upside on the board. Also the highest expectations.

Tulsa — The AAC Proving Ground

The pitch: A program with a tradition of punching above its weight. The American gives Castle a real conference platform and a clear path to a championship game if he builds it right.

The reality: Recruiting takes more grind here than in Florida, and the margin for error is thinner. But a coach who can develop overlooked talent can absolutely win — and win big — in Tulsa.

Castle's take: "Nobody believes in Tulsa? Good. I do my best work when the whole world's sleepin' on me. Also I heard they got great ribs."

Verdict: The builder's job. Perfect for a coach who wants to prove he can do it the hard way.

"Whichever one's coldest, baby."— Thad Castle, asked which program he'll pick

Coastal Carolina — The Culture Play

The pitch: Teal turf, national buzz, and a fanbase that already knows how to have fun. Coastal has shown it can shock the country. Hand the keys to a personality like Castle and the brand practically markets itself.

The reality: Sustaining success after the initial spark is the challenge. But if there's one place where Castle's swagger fits the culture like a glove, it's Conway.

Castle's take: "They play on TEAL grass. TEAL. I've never wanted anything more in my entire life. I'm already picturing the celebration."

Verdict: The best vibe match. Style and substance could collide beautifully here.

Colorado State — The Mountain West Rebuild

The pitch: A fresh canvas, a passionate community, and a conference wide open for a program willing to seize it. Fort Collins gives Castle room to build something entirely his own, brick by brick.

The reality: The longest road of the four. This is a multi-year project — but the coach who cracks the Mountain West gets to own a whole region.

Castle's take: "Mountains build character. You know what else builds character? Winnin' at altitude while the other team's gaspin' for air. Elevation is my sixth man, baby."

Verdict: The legacy play. Slow burn, big payoff.

The Bottom Line

Four schools. Four completely different bets. USF is the ceiling. Tulsa is the proving ground. Coastal is the perfect fit. Colorado State is the empire-building project.

Sources close to the program say Castle has narrowed his decision-making process down to "a gut feeling and a coin he found in his truck." Wherever he lands, one thing is certain: that program just got a whole lot louder. The only question left is which fanbase gets to hitch its wagon to him first.

Stay tuned, buddehs. This one's about to get real. 🏈
