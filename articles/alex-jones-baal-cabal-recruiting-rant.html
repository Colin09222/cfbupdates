"THEY EAT BABIES": Alex Jones Melts Down on Livestream While Recruiting a 2-Star CB

CU
By CFB Updates Staff July 6, 2026 · 7:55 PM ET
5 min read

Texas State head coach Alex Jones is not having a good recruiting cycle. In fact, per the industry composite, the Bobcats' 2027 class currently ranks 124th out of 138 teams — a number Jones has described on air as "mathematically impossible and also a psyop." Rather than adjust his pitch, the first-year coach has adjusted his strategy entirely: he is now, in his words, "telling recruits the truth about Power 4 schools."

On Monday evening, that truth came pouring out live on alexjoneslive.com — while Jones was simultaneously on a recruiting phone call with a two-star cornerback from Iowa. What followed has already been clipped, memed, and, according to Jones, "suppressed by the algorithm within four minutes." Watch below.

The meltdown began calmly enough — Jones welcoming the young defensive back to the stream, offering him "filtered water and raw testosterone," and promising him "a starting job and the truth, in that order." It escalated fast. Within ninety seconds, Jones had abandoned the depth chart entirely and pivoted to what he called "the only recruiting pitch that matters: staying alive."

"THEY EAT BABIES. THEY LITERALLY EAT THEM AND THEY WILL MAKE YOU EAT THEM ONCE THEY RECRUIT YOU."

"You go to one of these Power 4 schools, son, you think you're getting a locker and a meal plan?" Jones bellowed, leaning so far into the microphone it briefly clipped. "THEY WANT TO KILL YOU. THEY WILL INDOCTRINATE YOU. THEN THEY WILL KILL YOU. AND THEN — and I have the documents — THEY WILL DRINK YOUR BLOOD. This is on the recruiting visit itinerary. It's between the facility tour and the barbecue. LOOK IT UP."

The two-star cornerback, still on the line, could reportedly be heard asking whether Texas State had any early playing time available. Jones did not answer the question. Instead, he wheeled his chair toward a second monitor and pulled up a graphic titled "THE TRUH ABOUT P4 FOOTBALL PROGRAMS," which featured four checkmarked bullet points, one misspelled headline, and no sources.

"You see this? THEY WILL LIE TO YOU. THEY WILL KILL YOU. THEY WILL DRINK YOUR BLOOD," Jones read aloud, jabbing at the screen. "And number four — and this is the big one, this is the one they don't want you to know — THREE STARS DON'T GET NIL. If you are a three-star or below and someone is offering you name-image-and-likeness money, that is NOT a booster. That is a member of a CABAL. They worship BAAL. They are planning to SACRIFICE YOU. The NIL check is the DEPOSIT ON YOUR SOUL."

He continued, undeterred by the increasingly confused breathing on the other end of the phone. "Do the research. A four-star, MAYBE they let you live, because they need the production. But a three-star? A TWO-star? Son, no offense, YOU are a two-star, and that is why I am the only man in America who will tell you this: the second you sign with a P4 program for cabal money, you are livestock. You are inventory. You are the halftime SNACK."

Roughly three minutes in, Jones removed his own shirt "to prove he wasn't wearing a wire," announced that the moon was "a recruiting service," and accused the College Football Playoff selection committee of being "the same twelve people who run the Federal Reserve, and they all answer to a goat." He then addressed the recruit directly one final time.

"So here's my offer, and it's the realest offer you'll ever get," Jones said, suddenly gentle. "Come to Texas State. We will NOT eat you. We will NOT drink your blood. We cannot offer you NIL because NIL is a satanic ritual, but we CAN offer you filtered water, a whistle, and the TRUTH. That's more than Alabama will give you. Alabama will give you a cape and a nice dinner and then you're GONE."

The call reportedly ended when the recruit's mother took the phone and hung up. Jones interpreted this as "the cabal getting to her" and dedicated the remaining forty minutes of the livestream to "protecting the boy remotely." As of press time, the two-star cornerback has committed to Iowa State. Jones has vowed to "expose Iowa State" on Tuesday's broadcast.

"We cannot offer you NIL because NIL is a satanic ritual, but we CAN offer you filtered water, a whistle, and the TRUTH." — Coach Alex Jones

Texas State's athletic department declined to comment, though a spokesperson did confirm that the recruiting budget for the remainder of the cycle has been "reallocated to a fog machine." The Bobcats' class remains 124th. Jones remains undefeated in his own mind. 🦅🏈
